How to prepare...

Have any of the following documents, if you have access to them, to your meeting with an attorney.

  • Birth Certificate (if available)
  • Driver’s License (if available)
  • Passport (if available)
  • 1-94, Arrival-Departure Record
  • Green Card (if available)
  • Travel Documents
  • Work Permit
  • Letters to and from USCIS
  • Immigration Application
  • Judge’s Orders
  • Notices of Immigration Court Hearings
  • Notice to Appear in Immigration Court
